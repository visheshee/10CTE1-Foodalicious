What is foodalicious?

Foodalicious is your free, personal food tracker.

We offer personal pantry and fridge managment, so you can keep track of what you have and what you are missing.

Just add your ingredients and recipes and let us do the rest!

We will generate a shopping list for you based on your recipes and stock, so you can spend less time planning and more time cooking.

It is the perfect tool for anyone who loves food and wants to eat better, save money, and reduce waste.
